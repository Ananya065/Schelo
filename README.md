**Turn your to-do list into a journey.**

Schelo is a minimalist, beginner-friendly productivity tool that gamifies your daily tasks. Instead of a boring vertical list, Schelo visualizes your goals as a **path of milestones**. As you complete tasks, you move forward along the map toward your finish line.
